Eduardo Navarro Q

Painter & Sculptor

Curriculum

Eduardo Navarro Quelquejeu (Panama, 1960) has lived and worked in Panama City since 1970. Navarro has become well known for capturing the free spirit of horses with his neo-expressionistic action painting technique. Navarro has exhibited his works around the world, with international showings in Germany, Italy, Spain, Monaco, South Africa, USA, Colombia, Chile, Peru, Mexico, Argentina, China and Japan.
